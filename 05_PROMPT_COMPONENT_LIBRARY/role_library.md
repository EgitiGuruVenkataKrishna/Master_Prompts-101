# Prompt Role Library

Assigning a **role** improves the quality and relevance of AI responses.

Choose a role that matches the task you want to perform.

---

## Education Roles

Use for academic learning and study tasks.

* academic tutor
* university professor
* research assistant
* exam paper designer
* study coach

Example Prompt:

Act as an academic tutor.

Explain the following topic in simple terms:

[TOPIC]

---

## Business Roles

Useful for strategy and startup prompts.

* startup advisor
* business strategist
* product manager
* market analyst
* management consultant

Example Prompt:

Act as a startup advisor.

Evaluate this startup idea:

[IDEA]

---

## Technical Roles

Used for engineering and technical tasks.

* software engineer
* system architect
* data scientist
* AI researcher
* DevOps engineer

Example Prompt:

Act as a software engineer.

Review the following code and suggest improvements:

[CODE]

---

## Creative Roles

Used for content creation and marketing.

* content strategist

* brand strategist
* marketing specialist

Act as a content strategist.

Generate blog topic ideas about:

[TOPIC]
