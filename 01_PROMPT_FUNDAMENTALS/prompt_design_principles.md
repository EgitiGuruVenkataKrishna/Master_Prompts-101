# Prompt Design Principles

Good prompts follow clear principles.

---

## 1. Assign a Role

Example:

Act as a **startup advisor**

or

Act as a **university professor**

This improves answer quality.

---

## 2. Define Clear Objectives

Bad prompt:

Explain AI.

Good prompt:

Explain **machine learning concepts for beginners**.

---

## 3. Provide Context

Context helps the model understand your situation.

Example:

I am a **computer science student preparing for exams**.

---

## 4. Specify Output Format

Example:

Output as:

* bullet points
* table
* step-by-step explanation

---

## 5. Add Constraints

Example:

* keep response under 200 words
* explain for beginners
* include examples

---

## Key Idea

Clear instructions = better responses.
